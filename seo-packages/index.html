Our Requirement & Conditions

  • The client will change the URLs after the consultation form our end.
  • All the Meta tags i.e. title or description should not be altered without our consultation.
  • If opting for on-page SEO services, the client must grant complete access to the site including Admin and cPanel access to make alteration according to the requirements.
  • Payment needs to be done on time within the given due date.
  • Content should not contain any plagiarism if being provided by the client.
  • For custom SEO package, monthly charges will vary according to the requirement.
  • Keywords difficulty should vary from easy to super hard; if all the keywords with super hard difficulty are to be ranked, the price will be decided on a mutual discussion.
  • Minimum Contract Duration: 6 Months for old site and 12 months for new site.
